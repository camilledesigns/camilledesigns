About Me

Hi! I'm Camille Beltran, a UX Designer and recent UCSD grad. While at UCSD, I studied Cognitive Science, with a focus on design and interaction.

I was drawn to interaction design because of my love of working with people and interest in learning different thought processes. I also get so frustrated with technology which inspires me to make it better for own self. No matter what the reason, I am passionate about UX design and I thoroughly enjoy the iterative process that is design.
